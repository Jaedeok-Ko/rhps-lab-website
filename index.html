About the Lab

Who We Are

Founded in 2015, our laboratory is a hub of curiosity and collaboration. We bring together researchers from diverse disciplines — biology, chemistry, computational science, and engineering — united by a shared commitment to excellence and discovery.

Our approach is grounded in rigorous methodology and guided by the belief that fundamental research is the engine of transformative innovation. We collaborate with institutions worldwide, and our work has contributed to advances in medicine, materials science, and environmental sustainability.

We are proud to foster an inclusive environment where early-career scientists thrive alongside experienced researchers, forging the next generation of scientific leaders.

Research & Projects

Areas of Investigation

🔬

Molecular Biology

Investigating cellular mechanisms and gene regulatory networks to understand disease pathways and identify novel therapeutic targets.

⚗️

Materials Chemistry

Designing and characterizing advanced materials with tailored properties for energy storage, catalysis, and biomedical applications.

💻

Computational Modeling

Developing high-fidelity simulations and machine learning models to accelerate hypothesis testing and extract insight from complex datasets.

🌱

Environmental Science

Studying ecological systems and pollutant dynamics to inform evidence-based policies for environmental protection and restoration.

🧬

Genomics & Bioinformatics

Applying next-generation sequencing and computational genomics to map genetic variation and its functional consequences.

🤝

Translational Research

Bridging fundamental discoveries with clinical and industrial applications through close partnerships with hospitals and industry.
